## Room Availability Logic:
In order to facilitate a performant system when checking for room availability and ensuring that rooms are not double booked the following approach should be taken:

When a room is created a record should be created in the RoomAvailabity table, from the date of creation until the latest possible date.

When a booking is made against a room the record should be updated to reflect the new availability. This may mean modifying the current record and adding a new record.

Searching for available rooms for a given date can be done by comparing the dates against the availability records (and required capacity).
